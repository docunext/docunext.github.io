SMTP is used for outgoing and relayed email. The acronym stands for "Simple Mail Transfer Protocol". ### Simple or Enhanced? In fact, SMTP is *incredibly* simple. So simple that requirements necessitated an enhanced version, ESMTP, which includes support for users to authenticate prior to sending email. ### SMTP Specifications SMTP typically uses port 25, but due to overwhelming amounts of [[/Spam]], many ISPs block outgoing connections on port 25, forcing SMTP service clients to seek alternatives, such as port 587. ### SMTP Servers * [[/Postfix]]
