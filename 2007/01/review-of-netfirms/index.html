Review of Netfirms

January 21st, 2007

Netfirms ran a promotion I could not say no to: 2 domains and a year of hosting for $10!

January 19, 2007 Netfirms hosted domain is non-responsive. Receiving a timed out error. In a few hours the website was again reachable, not sure how long it took though.

NetFirms Commerce Pro

Netfirms offers osCommerce, which is a surprisingly complex and sophisticated piece of software. I'm not sure how many Netfirms customers actually use it, but its nice to know that it is available.

Database Connections Today (January 21, 2007), I've had two database connections fail. Upon refresh, the connection was successful.
